## About

I'm completing a B.Sc. in Computer Science *(Artificial Intelligence)* at **Helwan University** (expected 2027) and working as a **Machine Learning Intern at DEPI**, where I build deep learning systems end-to-end — from architecture selection through interpretability and deployment.

My focus is **Computer Vision and Explainable AI**, with a concentration in **medical imaging**: models that don't just classify, but generate visual explanations for their predictions. My flagship project, **Sunu AI**, pairs a fine-tuned DenseNet121 classifier with Grad-CAM visual explanations and an LLM-based clinical assistant.

As a secondary, deliberate specialization, I build backend systems in **ASP.NET Core** — so my models aren't confined to notebooks. This lets me take a project from research through to a working, deployable product.
